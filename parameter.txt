Face Changer ONNX commandline parameters

required:

--input "path_to_input.mp4"
--output "path_to_output.mp4"

optional:

--filter "path_to_filter.json"
--save "path_to_save_filter.json"

--audio
(keep audio)

--startpos frame_number
--endpos frame_number
(cut in/out source video)

--size 512
(default 256 = internal processing size, 256(faster) or 512(higher quality))

--scale 10
(rescale video 1 ... 20 / default 10 = original,  5 = half size, 20 = double size)
